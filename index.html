Advertising Information

Why Ads?

INFINITY Player is a free service. Ads help cover:

  • Hosting and server costs
  • Development time and maintenance
  • Future feature development
  • Keeping the service free for all users

Privacy & Ads

We use Google AdSense for advertising because:

  • It's a trusted Google service
  • Ads are reviewed for quality
  • You can control ad personalization
  • We don't collect personal data for ads

All ads are served by Google, not directly by us.

Ad Policies

  • No intrusive pop-up ads
  • No ads that block video content
  • No autoplay video ads with sound
  • Respect for user experience

If you see an inappropriate ad, please report it using the "Report Ad" button.

Supporting Without Ads

If you prefer no ads, you can:

  • Use an ad blocker (but please whitelist us if you can)
  • Make a donation to support development
  • Share the player with others
  • Provide feedback to improve the service
