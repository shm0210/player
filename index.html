💡 About INFINITY YouTube Player

INFINITY YouTube Player was created with one main goal — to help you study and focus without distractions.

Watching videos on YouTube often comes with ads, pop-ups, and distracting recommended videos. This player removes all of that so you can focus on what matters.

Using YouTube’s privacy-enhanced “nocookie” mode, videos load directly without saving personal data or tracking cookies.

No targeted ads. No distractions. Just clean, uninterrupted video playback for studying or learning.
